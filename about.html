About

The Skin 101 was established in 2004 by Dr. Jennie Francisco-Diaz and Dr. Oliver Diaz to make skin care readily available for everyone. It was built upon the belief that skin care must be made a priority. For 16 years and counting, every effort being made by the clinic—starting with being one of the first few to open in malls—was done with that in mind. With their fully equipped clinics and board-certified doctors, Skin 101 is able to accurately address and assess one's skin and body concerns.
